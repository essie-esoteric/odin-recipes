Chili

Who doesn't love a good bowl of chilli?! This has been my go-to recipe for over a decade now and you can use whatever kind of beans you'd like. I prefer dark kidney beans:

Ingredients:

  • 2lbs ground beef
  • 2 yellow onions
  • 1 red pepper
  • 6 garlic cloves
  • 3 jalape√±o pepper, large
  • 3 serrano peppers OR 2-3 habaneros
  • 2 cans diced tomatoes
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 3 cans dark kidney beans
  • 1/4 cup chili powder
  • 1 tsp salt

Steps:

  1. dice up onions, peppers and cloves
  2. brown the meat
  3. put all everything in crock pot
  4. let cook for several hours (8-10 hours) on high heat
  5. you got yourself some chili
