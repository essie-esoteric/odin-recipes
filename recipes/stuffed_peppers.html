Stuffed Peppers

Stuffed bell peppers filled with a mixture consisting of beef, rice, tomato sauce, onions and garlic and topped with melted cheese, this simple recipe is nutritious and delicious in addition to being easy to reheat:

Ingredients:

  • 4 bell peppers
  • 1lb beef
  • 2 Tbsp diced onion
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 minced garlic clove
  • 15oz can of tomato sauce
  • 1 tsp salt
  • shredded cheese to taste

Steps:

  1. hollow out 4 bell peppers from the top and blanch for 2-3 minutes, then set on paper towel to dry
  2. cook beef over medium heat until browned
  3. saute onion and garlic
  4. drain grease from beef pan and add the onion, rice, garlic, tomato sauce and salt. Cook roughly 5 minutes or until hot
  5. preheat oven to 350Â°F
  6. fill peppers with mixture
  7. bake 15-20 minutes, add cheese and put back in oven until cheese is melted
